Shey you sabi ?




Campaign Title: Shey you sabi ?

Client: Playhouse (Internal)


Background

How do we educate companies in Nigeria on the importance of digital media in an engaging way – while also raising our profile as an agency and showcasing ourselves as experts in digital?

What was/is our idea ?

We looked to piggy back on the popularity of the “Did you know?” series of animations but creating a localized Nigerian version titled Se You Sabi (Did You Know).

The idea is to disseminate interesting local facts about the digital landscape in Nigeria.
